Lemon-Pepper Zucchini Pasta With Dill

A summer favorite, zucchini bulks up raw salads, lends texture to muffins and bread, and adds a subtle salinity to pasta dishes. That's the role it plays in this simple miso butter pasta. A little pasta water and spoonfuls of miso melt into a delightful sauce to coat the noodles. Searing the delicate squash and tossing it with lemon zest and black pepper, then with the pasta allows zucchini to do what it does best: absorb all the flavors of the elements around it. This dish provides a playful riot of balanced yet contrasting flavors for a quick side dish or an easy dinner.

INGREDIENTS
Yield: 4 servings
Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 pound chicken breast, tenderized to 1-inch thickness
  • 2 medium zucchini, cut into ½-inch-thick half-moons
  • 1 lemon, entire lemon zested
  • ¼ cup chopped dill, plus more for garnish
  • 1 pound spaghetti or linguine
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons white miso
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup pecorino romano or parmesan cheese, finely grated
PREPARATION
Step 1: Prepare the Chicken
Pat chicken dry. Pound to even thickness or slice horizontally into thinner cutlets. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
Step 2
Bring salted water to boil. Salt zucchini with ½ tsp kosher salt, toss and let sit 10-30 minutes to draw out moisture.
Step 3
Zest entire lemon into bowl, add dill and ¼ tsp pepper. Mix to combine. Cut lemon into wedges.
Step 4
Cook pasta until al dente. Reserve 1½ cups pasta water, then drain.
Step 5: Cook the Chicken
Heat large skillet over medium-high, add 1 tbsp oil. Sear chicken 5-7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through (165°F). Rest, then slice. Keep fond in skillet.
Step 6: Cook the Zucchini
Pat zucchini dry. Heat same skillet over high, add remaining oil. Cook zucchini undisturbed 2-4 minutes until browned, flip and cook 1 minute more. Transfer to lemon-dill bowl and toss.
Step 7: Bring It All Together
Reduce heat to medium, add miso, butter, garlic and pasta water. Whisk until miso dissolves and simmering, scraping up browned bits, 1-3 minutes. Add pasta, cheese, zucchini mixture, and chicken. Toss until cheese melts and sauce coats everything. Garnish with dill and pepper, serve with lemon wedges.
